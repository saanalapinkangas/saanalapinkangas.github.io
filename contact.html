about me

Saana Lapinkangas

Hello! I'm Saana, a multi-talented creative born in 1991 in Oulu, Finland. I am currently studying software design at Oulu University of Applied Sciences. I started in the fall of 2022, and I am particularly interested in web design, where I can leverage my strong graphic design skills. I studied graphic design at the University of Lapland's Faculty of Arts for a couple of years.

I have been a full-time entrepreneur from 2016 to 2022. In my business, Lapinkettu (sole proprietorship/Ltd.), I created plywood jewelry, woodburning engravings, and art products as part of my own collection and customer orders. Entrepreneurship has taught me a lot and provided me with valuable experiences, but it was time to explore other opportunities. You can find a comprehensive history of Lapinkettu in the portfolio section.

In my free time, I spend time with my small family, enjoy outdoor activities, engage in various projects, and search for captivating TV series and audiobooks.

Skills

I am particularly interested and motivated to learn about the web environment (UI/UX) and front-end applications that are close to customers. For me, usability and logic combined with visual design are crucial in software development.

I am looking for internship and summer job opportunities. I value openness, support for employees, a positive work atmosphere, and flexibility within a company.

Work Experience

During my years as an entrepreneur, I have gained extensive experience in customer service, both in-person and through communication. I have received excellent feedback, especially in customer-oriented tasks, for my communication and understanding of customers' needs leading to successful outcomes. Additionally, I have experience in subcontracting, product design, corporate identity creation, sales, and leveraging social media. I am proficient in using Adobe software.

As an employee, I am self-motivated and productive. I am creative, enjoy variety, and get along with people of all kinds. I may not be the loudest in the room, but I express my opinions and, when necessary, can assert myself, for example, in public speaking situations.
